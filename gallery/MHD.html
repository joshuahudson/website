Example Magnetohydrodynamic Flow

Behind this text is a visualization of a two dimensional magnetohydrodynamic flow. To get a better look, scroll down or click the visualization in the menu on the right.

Each dot represents a particle of the fluid, the motion of which is dictated by the velocity field of the fluid, indicated by an arrow. The fluid is conductive, and influenced by a magnetic field to which it is coupled. Both the fluid and magnetic field are acted upon by simple external forces, supported on 2 modes.
